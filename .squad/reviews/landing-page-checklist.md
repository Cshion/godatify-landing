# B2B Consulting Landing Page Checklist

> Essential elements for a high-converting consulting company landing page

---

## 1. Above the Fold (Hero)

| Element | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| ✅ Clear value proposition | Communicate what you do in <5 seconds | Critical |
| ✅ Professional imagery/visual | Establish credibility immediately | Critical |
| ✅ Primary CTA | "Schedule consultation" / "Get Started" | Critical |
| ⚠️ Trust indicators | Client logos, certifications visible | High |
| ⚠️ Secondary CTA | "Learn more" for non-ready visitors | Medium |

**Best Practices:**
- Headline should address client pain point, not company feature
- CTA button should contrast with hero background
- Keep text minimal - 1 headline, 1 subheadline, 1-2 CTAs max

---

## 2. Social Proof

| Element | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| ✅ Client logos | Immediate trust signal | Critical |
| ✅ Testimonials | Peer validation | Critical |
| ✅ Case study highlights | Prove results | High |
| ⚠️ Industry certifications | Domain authority | Medium |
| ❌ Awards/recognition | Third-party validation | Medium |
| ❌ Media mentions | Credibility amplifier | Low |

**Best Practices:**
- Show logos of recognizable clients (aim for 5-8)
- Video testimonials outperform text 3:1
- Include specific metrics in case studies ("increased revenue 40%")

---

## 3. Services Section

| Element | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| ✅ Clear service offerings | What you deliver | Critical |
| ✅ Icons/visuals per service | Scannable format | High |
| ⚠️ Benefits focus (not just features) | "Why" not just "what" | High |
| ⚠️ Service-specific CTAs | Individual conversion paths | Medium |
| ❌ Pricing indicators | Set expectations | Low-Medium |

**Best Practices:**
- Lead with outcomes: "Reduce costs by 30%" not "Cost optimization service"
- 3-6 services optimal for homepage
- Each service should link to detailed page

---

## 4. About/Team Section

| Element | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| ✅ Company story/positioning | Emotional connection | High |
| ⚠️ Team expertise highlights | Human element | Medium |
| ⚠️ Years of experience stat | Trust building | High |
| ❌ Team photos | Personal connection | Medium |
| ❌ Founder story | Mission alignment | Low |

**Best Practices:**
- Focus on client-centric narrative ("We help X achieve Y")
- Stats should be specific and impressive (years, clients served, projects)
- Link to full About page for deeper content

---

## 5. Case Studies/Results

| Element | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| ✅ Concrete metrics | Prove ROI | Critical |
| ✅ Industry relevance | "They work with companies like mine" | High |
| ⚠️ Before/after narrative | Transformation story | High |
| ⚠️ Challenge → Solution → Result | Problem-solving proof | High |
| ❌ Client quotes within cases | Authenticity | Medium |

**Best Practices:**
- Lead with the result: "40% revenue increase for [Client]"
- Feature 3-6 cases covering different industries
- Include client industry/size for relatability

---

## 6. Contact/CTA Section

| Element | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| ✅ Contact form | Direct conversion | Critical |
| ⚠️ Multiple contact options | Phone, email, chat | High |
| ⚠️ Clear next steps | What happens after submission | High |
| ❌ Calendar scheduler | Reduce friction | High |
| ❌ Response time expectation | Set expectations | Medium |

**Best Practices:**
- Form fields: Name, Email, Company, Message (4 fields max for homepage)
- Include phone number visibly (B2B decision-makers prefer calls)
- Add "What to expect" copy below form

---

## 7. Trust Elements

| Element | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| ✅ Client testimonials | Peer trust | Critical |
| ⚠️ Security/privacy badges | Data confidence | Medium |
| ❌ Industry awards | Third-party validation | Medium |
| ❌ Certifications (ISO, etc.) | Professional standards | Medium |
| ❌ Partner logos | Association credibility | Low |

---

## 8. Technical/UX Requirements

| Element | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| ✅ Mobile responsive | 50%+ traffic is mobile | Critical |
| ✅ Fast load time (<3s) | SEO + UX | Critical |
| ⚠️ Clear navigation | Findability | High |
| ⚠️ Sticky header with CTA | Persistent conversion | High |
| ⚠️ Skip-to-content link | Accessibility | Medium |
| ❌ Live chat widget | Immediate engagement | Medium |
| ❌ Cookie consent | Compliance | Required |
